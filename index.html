about us

our story

Welcome to Mangata & Gallo, your premier destination for exquisite jewelry designed to celebrate life's most cherished moments. Specializing in engagements, weddings, and anniversaries, we offer a curated selection of high-quality, classic pieces that embody timeless elegance. Founded by Mariana, a talented Austin local with a passion for jewelry design, Mangata & Gallo began as a small boutique in Austin, Texas. Mariana's expertise in diamond cutting and metal smithing, honed through her design school education, has shaped the store's distinct style and reputation for excellence. Our store operates primarily online, with a charming storefront and atelier in Austin where you can explore our collection in person. Mariana continues to oversee every aspect of the business
